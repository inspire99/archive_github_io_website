Collection of links related to NLP and other stuff

( Disclaimer: This is a work in progress )

Just was looking around some stuff with NLP, bumped into following :


  1. NLP Architect from Intel - https://www.analyticsvidhya.com/blog/2018/05/nlp-architect-an-awesome-open-source-nlp-python-library-from-intel-ai-lab-with-github-link/
  2. Check their documentation from http://nlp_architect.nervanasys.com/
  3. Facebook had released PyText - https://code.fb.com/ai-research/pytext-open-source-nlp-framework/
  4. Good article about different NLP Tools to try - https://medium.com/microsoftazure/7-amazing-open-source-nlp-tools-to-try-with-notebooks-in-2019-c9eec058d9f1
  5. Related to chatbots - What is Microsoft LUIS - https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/cognitive-services/luis/what-is-luis?WT.mc_id=blog-medium-abornst
  6. Just to become aware of different NLP models present - https://blogs.technet.microsoft.com/machinelearning/2018/04/25/transfer-learning-for-text-using-deep-learning-virtual-machine-dlvm/
  7. This huggingface company is working on social AI ( bot trying to be like a friend ) . Check their achievement in coreference resolution - https://huggingface.co/coref/?text=I%20am%20going%20to%20New%20york%20city
  8. Spacy - Industrial NLP - Highly performant - https://spacy.io/models#architecture
  9. Thinc - ML library powering Spacy - https://github.com/explosion/thinc
